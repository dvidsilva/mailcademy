Welcome to IdeaJot

The best place to jot your ideas, or Sparks as we call them. A Spark is an idea or note and possibly a photo to help you remember it. You can edit your Sparks anytime on any device. Let's add your first Spark.

Create a Spark

Enter the name of your Spark into this main input.
This is enough information to create a Spark. Let's give your Spark a description with more detail.
 And while you are at it let's add an image to help you remember what we are talking about. Now press or 'Enter' to add this new Spark to your Battery

Your First Spark!

On this page you can edit your Spark.
Click directly onto the title or description and start typing.
All of your changes are saved automatically as you type. Any Sparks created on this page are automatically added to this Spark to elaborate on your main idea. Let's add a 'child' Spark to this idea. Now let's open the menu and select your Battery

Your Battery

The Battery is where your Sparks are stored. In this view you see only the top level Sparks (Sparks with no parents). You can create a new Spark here and it will be saved in the Battery . Let's do that now. If you think that a Spark should be part of another set of ideas you can drag and drop it onto that Spark. Try it with the Spark we just created. If you delete a Spark it is sent to the Trash Bin . It is kept there until you empty your trash.
